## Контекст вселенной

**Kiarche Continuum War** — научно-фантастическая 4X-стратегия. Ти'Оны — аугментированные люди с мозговым имплантом Кешари. Новатор Тир — первый герой Ти'Онов, созданный без помощи Кешари. Символ независимости расы.

**Эпоха IV — Автономия (Пороховой век):** Ти'Оны больше не зависят от Кешари. Тир создал инновационный процессор — первую технологию Ти'Онов, не основанную на знаниях Кешари. Момент, когда ученик превзошёл учителя.

## Промпт (EN)

```
Kiarche Continuum War concept art — Ti'On innovator "Tir", Era IV: Autonomy (Gunpowder Age). A young humanoid of the Ti'On race — silver-toned skin with a bright metallic sheen, dark hair cut short and styled practically. He wears a practical engineer's jumpsuit in dark green with multiple tool pockets and utility straps — the clothing of a hands-on inventor, not a politician. On his temples, a modified neural interface — but unlike standard Keshari implants that glow green, Tir's modifications pulse with a distinctive violet light, his own personal improvements. His expression is confident and slightly defiant, eyes bright green with a challenging gaze. In his hands, he holds the Innovation Processor — a small crystalline cube with pulsing inner light, shifting colors as it generates new ideas. Background: Tir's secret laboratory — a hidden workshop built without Keshari oversight. Makeshift workbenches covered in prototype devices, half-assembled processors with exposed crystalline circuits, chalkboard walls covered in equations and diagrams, stolen Keshari components repurposed for original designs. The space is messy but brilliant — the workshop of someone who works fast and doesn't care about neatness. Warm orange lighting from improvised lamps, violet glow from the modified neural interface, scattered blue sparks from experimental equipment. The atmosphere conveys rebellion through innovation, the thrill of creating something truly new, and the tension between student and teacher. Digital painting, concept art style, character portrait, 16:9 aspect ratio.
```
